About GYMFIT

GYMFIT is a new web app developed focussing on helping you keep track off their dietary, workout and supplement history and progress. The app is web based so you can access it from any web browser; making it easily accessible wether you are on the go, in the gym or sat at home. TThis aims to help you achieve your fitness goals.



How GYMFIT Started

I noticed a lack of applications that offer users the high portability of using an app on any device or the functionality of recording every element of their fitness journey often results in people losing track of what they are doing. This can lead to poor optimisation of all these areas of fitness. To record all aspects of your fitness you often need multiple apps; making the whole process more tedious and boring. Therefore I decided to develop an app that people want to use and is easy to use.
